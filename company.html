about fgsp

Feemo Inc., is an IT Service company that is first established at March 2014 in Shinjuku District in Japan.

Years after, with its growth and dedication, in December 2017, Feemo Inc. opened another branch in Manila, Philippines that is now Feemo Global Solutions Philippines, Inc., with a growing service such as Web Consulting, Web and Graphic Design, Web Development, iOS App Development, SEO, Article Creation and DTP Design.

Today, we continue growing and increasing the number of team and services we could offer to satisfy the business needs of our clients.

VISION

To reach full potential of the web developers and graphic designers, to drive a new era of development, growth, productivity and to give more job opportunities, especially to people with special needs.

MISSION

With our positive stance throughout our work, FGSP assures its high quality and excellent service for the benefit of the company, employees and its clients.
